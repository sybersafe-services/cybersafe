How Cyberbullying Happens

In the digital world, bullying never sleeps. It happens in seconds, spreads in minutes, and follows you forever.

24/7 No escape
∞ Permanent record

Persistent

Digital devices let bullies reach you 24 hours a day. No safe space, no break.

🔒

Permanent

Posts, screenshots, and messages last forever. One click can haunt your future.

👀

Hard to Notice

Parents and teachers can't see it. It happens in private chats and hidden feeds.

COMMON METHODS

How Bullies Strike Online

📩
Abusive Messages
Threats, insults via DMs, texts, Snapchat, WhatsApp. 63% of cases.
📸
Embarrassing Posts
Mean comments, rumors, memes, photos shared publicly or in groups.
🕵️
Fake Accounts
Impersonation, catfishing, or anonymous harassment to humiliate.
🚫
Social Exclusion
Kicked from group chats, ignored in games, blocked to isolate you.
🔞
Image-Based Abuse
Sharing private photos/videos without consent. Often with threats.
🔁
Repeated Harassment
Cyberstalking across platforms, flaming, or denigration.
